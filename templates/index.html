About Arena Empire Ventures

Established on 23rd January 2019 under the Registration of Business Act 1956, Arena Empire Ventures is a 100% Bumiputera-owned company dedicated to delivering high-quality property and facility maintenance and management services across a wide range of industries in Malaysia.

Since our inception, we have steadily built a solid reputation as a reliable partner for organizations seeking to enhance operational efficiency, maintain regulatory compliance, and streamline their day-to-day operations through tailored maintenance solutions.
